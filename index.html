About Andre.Wav

Andre.Wav is the name behind Quality Sounds — a producer and audio engineer focused on building beats and mixes that actually hold up wherever they're played, from car speakers to club systems.

Every session runs the same way: get the idea down fast, then spend the real time making it sound right — balanced, loud in the right places, clean everywhere else. That's the whole philosophy behind Quality Sounds.
